About Me

I enjoy making stories with data and gaining insights from it.My passion for data started when I was a freelance web developer. As I continued taking projects as a freelance developer, data became more prominent in most of my projects. I worked for an Ad Tech Startup where I helped with getting data from their websites and loading same into their CRM.
So I was doing a lot of data cleaning and more of Extract, transform, load (ETL) job for the client. I decided to deep-dive and to gain advanced know-how by taking a course in Data Science and business analytics with University of Leicester which I completed in 2015.My personal projects were built using SQL , Azure data factory, AWS, frameworks in Python such as Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, TextBlob and Visualization tools like Tableau & PowerBI.
